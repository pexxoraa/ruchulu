Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Ruchulu collects, why, and how it's used.

What we collect. When you create an account or place an order, we collect your name, email, phone number, and delivery address(es). If you pay online, payment is processed directly by our payment partner (Razorpay) — we never see or store your full card details.

How we use it. To process and deliver your orders, send order updates and receipts, respond to support requests, and — only if you've opted in — send occasional newsletters about new products or festival offers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Who we share it with. Only the parties needed to fulfil your order: our courier partners (to deliver your package) and our payment gateway (to process online payments). Each is bound to use your data only for that purpose.

Cookies. We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your cart if you're browsing as a guest. We don't use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights. You can view and update your profile and addresses any time from your account, and can request that we delete your account and associated data by contacting us. We'll retain order records as required for tax and accounting purposes even after account deletion.

Data security. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing (never in plain text), and our systems use encrypted connections (HTTPS) throughout.

Changes to this policy. If this policy changes materially, we'll note the update here. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the revised policy.

Questions about your data? Reach out any time.
