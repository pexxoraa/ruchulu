Our Story

It started with one jar of avakaya, at ammamma's kitchen table.

What began as a grandmother sharing her mango pickle with neighbours has grown into a small collective of home cooks across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana — each one preserving a recipe passed down through their own family.

We don't run a factory. We run a network of kitchens, each responsible for the dishes they know best, so that every jar tastes like it came from someone's home — because it did.

"We don't sell pickle. We send home a little bit of Andhra."
